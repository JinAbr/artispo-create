Liam Reyes

Title: Ocean Dreams

Description: Watch as Liam Reyes brings the calming shades of the ocean to life, painting waves and reflections in beautiful blues. Perfect for lovers of seascapes and serene artwork.

Materials Used:

  • Acrylic paints (various shades of blue)
  • Canvas panel
  • Medium and large brushes

Tips:

  • Blend different shades of blue for depth and movement.
  • Use long, flowing brush strokes for smooth transitions.
  • Add subtle white highlights to mimic sunlight on waves.
