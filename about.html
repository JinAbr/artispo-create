We believe art is more than creation, it is building connection through expression. Artispo is a platform where one can browse art inspirations, acquire potential, and foster those into creative power.

Origin & Purpose

Origin and Purpose

The name Artispo is a combination of the term Art and/or Artist, and Inspiration. It is also short for the phrase 'Art is potential', and 'Art is power' in which reflects the objective of this platform.

The main purpose of this website is to empower the creative minds and environment to acquire and share inspiration that will help them thrive in their respective art fields.
